My Beautiful Girl

Poetry for You

I'm lost in your beautiful charm, Feeling a sincere and passionate love, Will you accompany my steps? Going through the day with one love.
Under the moon, I promise, To hold this feeling with all my heart, Nothing can change, A love that grows so beautifully.
With you, the world feels perfect, There's no need to search for meaning anymore, Because in your presence, I find everything, True love, within our souls.
